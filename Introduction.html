Introduction

A brief history about marine life existing a long time ago.

November 1, 2015 Belén López

First of all, let me introduce the first predator that ever existed: the Anomalocaris. This animal was similar to a crustacean and, since it was the first predator to ever exist, it had no competition in its time, begining like this the era of "the strongest and smartest wins".

Now, fast forward to the Mesozoic era, which began after the Permian-Triassic extinction; event that erased about 90% of the species. This new beginning gave as a result the appearance of dinosaurs, giant animals that began populating the globe. Mesozoic era is divided in three periods: Triassic, Jurassic and Cretaceous; giving birth to new species as lizards, birds ans animals and the dinosaurs we all know about, this respectively with said period.

Lastly, in the sea, an evolution was happening among the sea creatures. They began to develop different shell-chrushing behaviours and techniques which they finished perfecting on the Late Cretaceous. This made prey animals to evolve their defenses for their predators, however, not many species achieved this and got extinct.
